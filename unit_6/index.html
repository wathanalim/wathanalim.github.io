About

W. Lim Productions is a full-service creative studio that assists with all of your pre/post production needs, casting, location scouting, crew hiring, and fabrication needs for still and video shoots.

Based in California and Colorado we specialize in the west and west cost 'look'. We pride ourselves on assembling the best talent and making the impossible possible. Our in-house creative directors and content producers bring our client's visions to reality.
